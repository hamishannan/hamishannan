Frequently Asked Questions

How do online coaching sessions work?

Sessions are conducted via Zoom, allowing for face-to-face interaction regardless of your location. You'll receive a link to join at your scheduled time. All you need is a device with a camera, microphone, and stable internet connection.

What should I prepare before my first session?

For your first session, come prepared with specific goals or areas you'd like to work on. If you're preparing for an audition, have your monologue or sides ready. For public speaking coaching, an outline of your presentation is helpful.

Do you offer refunds if I need to cancel?

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before your scheduled session are eligible for a full refund or reschedule. Cancellations within 48 hours may be rescheduled but are not refundable.

How long will it take to see results?

While many students report immediate insights and improvements after just one session, significant growth typically comes with consistent practice and multiple sessions. The 3-session package is designed to provide comprehensive development.
