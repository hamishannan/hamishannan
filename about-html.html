ABOUT

HAMISH ANNAN

Hamish Annan is an award-winning interdisciplinary artist based in Auckland, New Zealand.

He is known for his work as an actor, writer, voiceover artist, acting coach, and performance artist. Hamish trained at The Actors' Program and holds a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and Theatre Studies.

With a focus on innovative storytelling and authentic performance, Hamish has created several successful solo works and collaborated with leading theatre companies across New Zealand and Australia.

ARTISTIC PRACTICE

Hamish's performance practice explores themes of human connection, vulnerability, and authentic expression through innovative theatrical forms that challenge traditional boundaries between performer and audience.

His works often incorporate elements of immersive theatre, autobiographical storytelling, and audience interaction to create unique experiences that resonate on both intellectual and emotional levels.
